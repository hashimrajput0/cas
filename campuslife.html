Campus Life at CAS

At CAS, we believe that learning extends far beyond the classroom. Our vibrant campus life offers students opportunities to explore their passions, develop new skills, and build lifelong friendships. From academic activities and sports to cultural events and community engagement, every day at CAS is filled with experiences that inspire growth, creativity, and leadership. We aim to create an environment where students feel motivated, supported, and proud to be part of the CAS family.

Cultural Day at CAS

"Cultural Day at CAS is a vibrant celebration of traditions, diversity, and unity. Students showcase colorful outfits, perform traditional songs and dances, and share the rich heritage of different cultures. Its a day filled with learning, laughter, and pride — reminding us that our differences are what make us stronger together.

A day when our campus bursts into color, music, and tradition. From traditional dresses to soulful performances, Cultural Day brings together the spirit of unity in diversity, making it one of the most memorable celebrations of the year.

Bonfire Night at CAS

An evening of warmth, laughter, and togetherness. Around the glowing fire, students enjoy music, games, and unforgettable conversations under the starry sky — creating memories that last long after the flames fade.

A night where the campus comes alive with music, laughter, and the crackle of the fire. Friends gather, share stories, and enjoy a cozy evening under the winter sky.

Games at CAS:

From powerful shots to roaring cheers, cricket at CAS is more than a game — it’s passion, teamwork, and school pride. Every match brings the thrill of competition and the joy of playing together.

Bat in hand, eyes on the ball, and hearts full of excitement — cricket is where CAS students show their skills, teamwork, and love for the game.

Farewell at CAS

A day of mixed emotions — celebrating achievements while saying goodbye. With heartfelt speeches, laughter, and memories shared, our farewell is a tribute to the journey and friendships that will always be cherished.
